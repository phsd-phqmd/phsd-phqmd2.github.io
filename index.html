Project Scope

The PHSD framework connects initial hard scatterings and string formation to deconfinement, QGP evolution, hadronization, and final hadronic interactions.

It is built on generalized off-shell transport equations and a Dynamical Quasi-Particle Model (DQPM) constrained by lattice QCD thermodynamics.

Applications range from SPS energies to RHIC and LHC observables, including spectra, anisotropic flow, and electromagnetic probes.
